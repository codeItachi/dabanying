“I Prefer This Colorful World”quoted line from a Chinese Song Dynasty poets Songwen“Congratulation to bridegroom in West Lake”.“回首洛阳花世界 , 烟渺黍离之地。”“One flower, One world”it's a philosophical implication that embodies the world is boundless, comprehensive and infinite. Our needs from money are strongly connect with our everyday life—the desire , the materials , the history and so on.
Fragments of word messages, portraites, letters, numbers, pattersn, white strips, are observed, rearranged and collaged to concentric circles.
